About us

Our story begins in February 2022 in the high school of Herculaneum where, thanks to a pendulum experiment organized by our physics teacher, we met. Within days of that meeting we decided we wanted to build a robot with the purpose of collecting cigarette butts. From then on we started designing, building, making mistakes but most of all learning. Driven by common values, we overcame a thousand obstacles and carried the project forward.

RoboButts MK 2

RoboButts MK 2 is a robot created and designed for the purpose of detecting and collecting cigarette butts. The idea came about mainly because of an environmental problem since cigarette butts are one of the most prevalent wastes on our planet and the longest to dispose of, in fact it takes up to 30 years for a single butt to be disposed of. The robot consists of a moving part and a robotic arm. This prototype is controlled via a pc keyboard but in the next one we are working on the robot will be fully autonomous

RoboButts MK 1

This was our first prototype that didn't work really well because of some mistake in the designing from which we learned a lot. So we decided to move to RoboButts MK 2
